      Methods, briefly

      Counters begin at the site’s launch and only ever go up. Percentages are rounded to whole numbers; any breakdown with fewer than 30 judgments stays unpublished until enough listeners weigh in. These are plain counters on a server — no per-player data exists, and none can be added. How clips are made, paired, and loudness-matched is on the About page.
